NH-METRO Account-Free Remittance?

  • "Without bank account, you can remit money to Metro Bank and Metro bank's agents in Philippines. The recipient can receive remittance by Pin-number and English name."

Eligibility and Remittance Purpose

  • Koreans : Remittance without submitting evidential documents
  • Foreigners : Remittance of Income/Pention etc.(To use this service, please visit a branch to apply for foreign transaction bank designation.)

Receiving Country

  • Philippines(PH)

Currency

  • USD(U.S. dollar), PHP(Philippine peso)

Sending Amount

  • Maximum USD3,000 in 1 day

Service Hours

  • This service is available between 00:30~24:00 every day.(Sat/Sun, Public holidays included)

The time required to remit

  • "Before 3 p.m. on banking business day, it takes about 4 hours to remit money. The recipient will be able to receive remittance as early as the same day."
  • After 3 p.m. on banking business day(including Sat/Sun, Public Holidays), it takes about a day to remit money. The recipient can receive remitance on the next banking business day.

Receiving Money in Philipphines

  • Locations for cash-pick-up : Metro Bank and Metro Bank's Agent
  • When receiving money, a receiver needs to present proper ID document and PIN-NUMBER.(PIN-NUMBER consists of 16 digits including the number and the English letters.)
  • The recipient will receive the entire remittance amount without any extra charge overseas.
