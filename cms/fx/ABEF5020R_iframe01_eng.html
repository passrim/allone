WU-AUTOSEND via AllOne Bank?

  • The Western Union AUTO-SEND Money TransferSM Service which can be used via NH Bank’s AllOne Bank after pre-registration of the required MT information (sender, receiver, receiving country etc.) at any of NH Bank branches.

WU-AUTOSEND?

  • The Western Union AUTO-SEND is the fast MT Service which is available for pick-up within 10 min. after sending at more than 500,000 Agent locations in over 200 countries and territories around the world.

Target Customers

  • This service is available only for those who pre-registered [ATM/Mobile WU-AUTOSEND] at any of NH Bank branches.
    - Customer needs to bring his/her ID, Passport, Proven documents etc. to register for this service. Foreign customer needs to pre-register NH Bank as a designated Foreign Exchange Transaction Bank.

Sending Amount

  • Maximum USD 3,000 per 1 Transaction, USD 5,000 in 1 day.

Transaction Purpose

  • Korean : Money Transfer that proof-of-payment is not required.
  • Foreigner : Money Transfer of Income/Pension etc.

Service Hours

  • ATM/Mobile AUTO-SEND is available from 04:00 to 23:00 every day(Sat/Sun, Public Holidays included)

Receiving Money Overseas

  • After the sending transaction via NH Bank’s AllOne Bank, a receiver can pick up money at any of Western Union Agent locations overseas within 10 min.
  • When receiving money, a receiver needs to present proper ID document and MT information (MTCN, Receiver Name, Sending Amount etc.)
