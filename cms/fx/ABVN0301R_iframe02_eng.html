[Important] Service Introduction and Cautions

  • This service is not applicable to 「The MOA FX Service」.
  • Once the remittance is made, any amendment or cancelation is impossible. Be sure to enter remittance info correctly.
  • NH Bank is not responsible for any damage from overdue remittance or remittance refund due to an error or incorrect data entry borne by you.
  • Please visit a nearest bank counter when a remittance info amendment or cancelation (money return) is needed.
    - Cancelation is allowed only when the amount has not been paid to the receiver yet.
  • When the money is to be returned due to an incorrect remittance info, the foreign exchange loss caused by the difference between the exchange rate at the time of remit (T/T selling rate) and at the time of returning (T/T buying rate) is not compensated. The remittance fee will not be returned either.
  • To use this service, the money should be withdrawn from the designated Korean Won bank account of the customer and the account must have been registered as your withdrawal account.
    - Withdrawal from Foreign Currency Savings Account or NH Retail Accounts is not possible.
    - Registration of the designated KRW Bank Account for AllOne Bank is possible at “Set-up>Registration/Cancellation of the AllOne Bank Account”.
  • When the remittance is made, the sender must send the relevant information (amount, receiver's name and PIN No. etc.) to the recipient in person. Be careful not to expose the info to others.
