[Important] Service Introduction & Cautions

  • This service is not applicable to 「The MOA FX Service」
  • Any changes or cancellation of processed transaction will not be allowed. Thus, customer needs to check the MT information details (sending amount, receiver information etc.) very carefully and confirm the transaction.
  • If customer would like to change the receiver information or request cancellation of the transaction, he/she needs to visit the NH Bank branches.
    • Cancelation is allowed only when the amount has not been paid to the receiver yet.
    • In case of cancellation, the repaying amount shall be converted into KRW at the customer TT rate on the pay date after deducting all the cost by the bank from the refunded amount from the original overseas country.
    • Sending fee is non-refundable.
  • To use this service, the money should be withdrawn from the designated Korean Won bank account of the customer and the account must have been registered as your withdrawal account.
    - Withdrawal from Foreign Currency Savings Account or NH Member's Cooperative Account is not possible.
    - Registration of the designated KRW Bank Account for AllOne Bank is possible at “Set-up>Registration/Cancellation of the AllOne Bank Account”.
  • Once the money transfer is completed, the sender should deliver the MT result and detailed information (MTCN, Sending Amount, Receiver Name etc.) to the receiver directly and should be careful not to expose it to the 3rd party.
