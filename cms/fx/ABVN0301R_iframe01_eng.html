NH-AGRI Account-Free Remittance?

  • You can remit money to AGRI bank in Vietnam using beneficiary’s PIN number and name. No account number is required. This service is available for only AGRI bank in Vietnam.

Eligibility and Remittance Purpose

  • Koreans : Remittance without submitting evidential documents
  • Foreigners : Remittance of salaries to home country
    (To use this service, please visit a branch to apply for foreign exchange transaction bank designation.)

Receiving Country

  • Vietnam (VN)

Currency

  • USD

Sending Amount

  • USD 3,000 or less/day

Service Hours

  • This service is available between 00:30~24:00 (including Saturdays, Sundays, & holidays)

Receiving Money Overseas

  • Once the application for remittance is received by AllOne Bank, the receiver receives the amount at AGRI BANK (Vietnam) within 24 hours.
  • When the transaction is made during non-service hours, the amount can be received on the next business day.
  • The receiver should submit his/her own identification and the transaction info(receiver name and PIN No.).
